Welcome to Landa's Cookbook

Introduction

Hi! My name is Yolanda Chen. I am a student at UCLA who loves cooking!

Cooking may be intimidating at first, but once you get the hang of it, cooking is actually very fun and interesting! In my website, you'll be able to find some of my favorite treats and meals to make for my family and friends. My goal is to record my recipes as well as provide some detailed explanations for each step to make it easier for beginners to follow along! Hopefully, you will enjoy going through my recipes. Also, please leave us a comment by using the "Contact us" button on the upper right corner if you tried my recipe! Any suggestions or stories will be greatly appreciated.
